Indian Airline Routes

An attempt to map all air routes in India

Sriharsha Devulapalli

The original goal of the project was to look at flight routes in India and see which ones see the most delays and how often do flights come on time, really. Unfortunately, that data is difficult. With what I have, I made a bunch of pretty maps.
