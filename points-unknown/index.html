About Me

My name is Sriharsha Devulapalli. You can call me Harsha (hur-shah). I'm currently studying Data Journalism at the Columbia University. I have worked as a researcher, geographer, urban designer, developer, and product manager. I enjoy maps, cities, data visualization, social justice, transit, languages, typography, and history.
